Impacts of the World Wide Web on Society

Invented in 1989, the world wide web has become a crucial part of society, revolutionising

  • Communication
  • How we work
  • Access to information
  • Entertainment

amoung countless other aspects of life.

The amount the world has changed since the invention of the WWW covers far more topics than I have time to discuss.
